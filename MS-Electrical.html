Dissertation

Title: Design of Metasurfaces for High-Performance Sensing and Absorption Applications.

I designed three highly sensitive all-dielectric metamaterial-based refractive index sensors, achieving a sensitivity of up to 798 nm/RIU and a figure of merit (FOM) of 732, using CST Studio. Later, I developed an ultrawideband absorber for solar thermophotovoltaics that achieves over 90% absorption between 300 nm and 1200 nm and an emitter with over 97% emission at 2.3 µm (aligned with the InGaAsSb bandgap). Simulated efficiency exceeded 20% under full solar concentration using CST Studio and COMSOL Multiphysics.
