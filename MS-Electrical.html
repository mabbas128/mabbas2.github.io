    • Dissertation: Design of Metasurfaces for high-performance sensing and absorption applications.

  • I designed three highly sensitive all-dielectric metamaterial-based refractive index sensors, achieving a sensitivity of up to 798 nm/RIU and a figure of merit (FOM) of 732, using CST Studio. After completing the sensing design, I shifted focus to solar thermophotovoltaics (STPV). In this area, I developed an ultrawideband absorber that achieves over 90% absorption in the range of 300 nm to 1200 nm, and an emitter with over 97% emission at 2.3 Î¼m, corresponding to the InGaAsSb bandgap energy. The system's simulated efficiency exceeds 20% under full solar concentration, and the designs were implemented using COMSOL Multiphysics and CST Studio.
