Introduction:¶

In recent years, there has been a notable surge in electric vehicle production. This growth in the EV industry is driven by several factors, including environmental benefits, lower operating costs, energy efficiency, government incentives, improved performance, energy independence, and the expansion of charging infrastructure.

In this project I plan to analyze a dataset containing information of electric vehicles in washington state to observe the trend of this grwoing industry and based on current market try to predict future of electric vehicles.

Project Proposal¶

I plan to analyze a dataset containing information on electric vehicles in Washington State.The dataset is obtained from link text.

import matplotlib.pyplot as plt
import pandas as pd

# Group data by company and model year, then count vehicles
company_model_year_counts = EV_df.groupby(['Make', 'Model Year'])['VIN (1-10)'].count().reset_index()
company_model_year_counts.rename(columns={'VIN (1-10)': 'Count'}, inplace=True)

# Get top 10 manufacturers
top_10_manufacturers = company_model_year_counts.groupby('Make')['Count'].sum().nlargest(10).index

# Filter data for top 10 manufacturers
filtered_data = company_model_year_counts[company_model_year_counts['Make'].isin(top_10_manufacturers)]

# Create plot
plt.figure(figsize=(12, 6))
for manufacturer in top_10_manufacturers:
    manufacturer_data = filtered_data[filtered_data['Make'] == manufacturer]
    plt.plot(manufacturer_data['Model Year'], manufacturer_data['Count'], marker='o', label=manufacturer)

plt.title('Number of Cars Produced by Top 10 Manufacturers Over Time')
plt.xlabel('Model Year')
plt.ylabel('Number of Cars Produced')
plt.grid(True)
plt.legend()
plt.show()

The above plot compares the over the time growth of production by top ten electric vehicle manufacturers.

import matplotlib.pyplot as plt

# Group data by company, model year, and get maximum electric range
company_model_year_max_range = EV_df.groupby(['Make', 'Model Year'])['Electric Range'].max().reset_index()

# Get top 10 manufacturers based on total electric range
top_10_manufacturers = company_model_year_max_range.groupby('Make')['Electric Range'].sum().nlargest(10).index

# Filter data for top 10 manufacturers
filtered_data = company_model_year_max_range[company_model_year_max_range['Make'].isin(top_10_manufacturers)]
