Final Year Project

My final year project, “Smart Panel Monitoring System”, aimed to remotely monitor electrical parameters of a building—such as per-phase voltage, current, power factor, load distribution, and total harmonic distortion. The system was designed to trigger SMS and email alerts during critical events.

  • Installed a three-phase energy meter on the main electrical panel
  • Used open-source software Modbus Poll to extract real-time data i.e., line Voltage, line current, power factor etc.
  • Developed a web interface for data display and management
  • Integrated third-party APIs for mobile alerts and email notifications in case of any problems
