    • Final Year Project

  • My final year project was “Smart Panel Monitoring System” the goal of the project was to develop a system for remote monitoring of electrical data of a building e.g. per phase voltage, per phase current, power factor, load distribution, Total harmonic distortion, etc. and to generate an email and mobile alert in case of an emergency. To achieve the results we installed a three-phase energy meter on the main electrical panel of the building, extracted its data using an open-source software “Modbus poll”, created a website, and managed all the data retrieved by the energy meter on that website. Moreover, we used APIs for mobile SMS and email alerts.
