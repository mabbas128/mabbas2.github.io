Dissertation

Title: Optoelectronic Properties of Stacked CVD-grown MoS2 Monolayers for High-Performance 2D Material-Based Photovoltaics

  • Developed stacked CVD-grown MoS2 monolayers to enhance light absorption while maintaining their intrinsic properties.
  • Performed extensive material characterization including PLQY, Raman spectroscopy, and Hall measurements.
  • Designed and fabricated scalable Schottky junction photovoltaic devices with vertical carrier transport architectures.
  • Implemented a novel mesh transfer method for precise film alignment on PV devices.
  • Analyzed photovoltaic performance using IV curves, EQE, and IQE metrics.
